7 /**
8  * Encapsulates the data sent to the server during TCP communication.
9  *
10  * <p>
11  * This data contains all the information acquired from the
12  * {@link project.android.MouseFragment}, the
13  * {@link project.android.TouchpadFragment} and the
14  * {@link project.android.KeyboardFragment}. It contains a
15  * <i>type of operation</i> field which helps the server determine
16  * the operation requested by the client and a <i>data</i> field
17  * which acts as the operand for the input operations.
18  * </p>
19  * <p>
20  * For keyboard input, the <i>type of operation</i> field contains
21  * <code>Key</code> and the <i>data</i> field contains the sequence
22  * of characters and keyboard strokes for input. In case of special
23  * keys, the <code>data</code> field contains the special key type.
24  * </p>
25  * <p>
26  * For 3-D mouse movement operation, the <i>type of operation</i>
27  * field contains <code>Mouse_Move</code> and the <i>data</i> field
28  * contains a {@link project.android.sensor.representation.Quaternion}
29  * object.
30  * </p>
31  * <p>
32  * For 2-D mouse movement operation, the <i>type of operation</i>
33  * field contains <code>Mouse_Touch</code> and the <i>data</i> field
34  * contains a pair of integers representing the displacement along the
35  * x-axis and y-axis relative to the current mouse pointer location.
36  * </p>
37  * <p>
38  * For mouse button click operation, the <i>type of operation</i>
39  * field contains <code>Mouse_Button</code> and the <i>data</i> field
40  * contains any of the following :
41  * <ul>
42  * <li><code>left</code> - representing left click.</li>
43  * <li><code>right</code> - representing right click.</li>
44  * <li><code>middle</code> - representing middle button click.</li>
45  * <li><code>upscroll</code> - representing scroll up.</li>
46  * <li><code>downscroll</code> - representing scroll down.</li>
47  * </ul>
48  * </p>
49  *
50  * @see project.android.sensor.representation.Quaternion
51  * @see project.android.MouseFragment
52  * @see project.android.TouchpadFragment
53  * @see project.android.KeyboardFragment
54  */
